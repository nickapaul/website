Self-Hosting and Home Automation

Take back control of your digital life. Most modern services rely on "The Cloud," which is often just a fancy way of saying "someone else’s computer." I specialize in designing and implementing custom self-hosted solutions that put your data back where it belongs: on hardware you own, stored securely in your own home. By removing the middleman, you gain unmatched performance and the peace of mind that comes with true data privacy.

Stop paying for subscriptions you can own. Beyond privacy, self-hosting is a powerful way to eliminate recurring monthly fees. From replacing costly cloud storage with a personal home media server to installing private camera security systems that don't require a "gatekeeper" fee, the savings add up fast. I help you navigate the complexity of the initial setup, turning your home into a high-performance, self-sufficient ecosystem.

My self-hosting and automation services follow a transparent, project-based pricing model. We begin with a consultation and site survey to determine your specific needs—whether it’s a whole-home media server or a private security network.

I provide a detailed implementation plan and cost estimate upfront, covering the hardware requirements and my professional setup fee. Throughout the build, I maintain open communication regarding any technical adjustments, ensuring the final result is a turnkey solution that is fully tested and ready for your family to use.
