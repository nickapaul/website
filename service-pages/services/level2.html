Level 2 Support

Imagine you’re mid-task and your PC suddenly goes dark. Or perhaps you’ve finally sat down to relax, but your system won’t power on at all. When the screen stays black or the hardware fails, you need more than a quick tip—you need Level 2 Hardware Support.

This ad-hoc service is designed for critical failures, including component diagnostics, hardware repairs, and full system upgrades. Whether it’s a dead power supply or a motherboard that won't post, I provide the professional-grade intervention needed to get your hardware back online.

My Level 2 service is built on transparency and precision. Once I receive your system, I perform a comprehensive diagnostic to pinpoint the failure and provide a clear repair estimate before any work begins.

I believe in "no surprises" service; if the scope of the repair changes, I will contact you promptly to discuss the best path forward. Once the hardware is tested and the job is complete, I provide a final itemized bill for a seamless checkout. You stay in control of the process from the first diagnostic to the final power-on.
